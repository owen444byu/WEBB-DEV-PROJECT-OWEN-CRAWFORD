Four Reasons Why I love Golf

  1. It teaches focus
    • You need to be patient
    • You have to execute many aspects of a swing simultaneously
  2. Connects people
    • Meet new people on the golf course playing in groups of 4
    • You can connect with the employees
    • You can travel the world and play many courses
  3. The feeling of a clean shot
    • When the ball flies exactly how you planned
    • The feeling at impact
    • The energy transfer from the club to the ball
  4. Connection
    • I can play with my family and connect with them
    • You can connect with nature
    • You can connect with a sport

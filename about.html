About Me

Hi, I’m Owen Crawford — a pre-business student at BYU’s Marriott School of Business with a growing passion for healthcare leadership. My background spans industries from golf course operations to fitness companies, where I’ve learned the value of teamwork, clear communication, and hard work. Having been an intern at WebbWell, Field Expert at Moxie, and Greens Keeper at Buffalo Run, I have learned skills that make me a valuable asset to a company. I am driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact in someone's life. I am also fluent in Spanish and have a love for the game of golf. I’m excited to bring my passion, experience, and drive to a career where people come first and where we can make a difference.
